About Us!

Welcome To The Sparks Foundation

Founded in 2008, The Sparks Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing educational opportunities for underprivileged students in India. Through initiatives like scholarships, mentorship, after-school programs, computer labs, and teacher training, The Sparks Foundation has positively impacted the lives of thousands of children, enabling them to stay in school, excel academically, and develop essential skills for success in life. Awarded with the Prime Minister's Award for Social Innovation and the National Award for Excellence in Education, The Sparks Foundation's impact continues to inspire, making a true difference in the lives of those in need.
